Personal Development

Every employer look for attributes in graduates which are also known as the soft skills. These soft skills include:-

  • Critical Thinking and Problem Solving
  • Professionalism
  • Communication Skills
  • Teamwork and Leadership
  • Learning and Research Skills

Critical Thinking and Problem Solving

Critical thinking is a skill in which an individual gathers information about a topic and then he looks into both the advantages and the disadvantages of that situation or the subject. After learning and looking into both cons and pros, the individual then makes an evaluation and ends with making a conclusion. Looking into both cons and pros to make a decision is known as the Critical Thinking. There have been a few times when i had to think critically, for example, when i had decide which university i should go, i went for open days of a few universities. I chose to study in Manchester Metropolitan University because it is situated in the centre of the city and all the facilities are available. Facilities such as public transport, restaurants, accommodation, Leisure centres and many more. In other universities, i learned about the area and compared to MMU, there were less facilities available.

Problem Solving skill is an essential skill that a graduate must possess. At work you can face many problems and issues, some of them are expected while others may be unexpected. If a problem arises, you cannot just stop and wait for the problem to be resolved by itself, you have to solve the problem and move forward by assessing the situation. I find myself capable of solving the problems. In computing, we face a number of problems and you have to solve them by your own. For example, in programming, you may see a problem that needs to be resolved.

Professionalism

It is important to be professional at workplace. To be professional you need to have the attributes such as accountability, consideration, communication etc. The employers look for individuals with professional behaviours because these individuals are best for following the codes of conducts in the workplace. This is a skill that i have to work on.

Communication Skills

To undertake a task at workplace, you have to communicate with other people, understand what they are saying and if you disagree with something then putting your point of view forward. For all this, you have to be good at communication which includes speaking, reading, listening and writing. I find sometimes difficult to understand people with a different accent. Other than that i am able to communicate with people. At my workplace, working as a security officer includes a lot of communication with managers, security officers, customers, police and many other emmergency services as well.

Teamwork and Leadership

Teamwork and Leadership are crucial to be employed. As a security officer, we have to work in a team to protect the stock and if someone needs to be stopped. We communicate with each other and keep updating about what is our target and what we are looking at. Without the teamwork it is hard to undertake the tasks. Leading the team is another skill that the employers look for.
